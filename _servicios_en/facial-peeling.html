Benefits of Facial Peeling

  • Eliminates spots and improves texture.
  • Stimulates skin regeneration.
  • Reduces acne scars and other imperfections.

Treatment Details

  • Duration: Approximately 1 hour.
  • Anesthesia: Not required.
  • Visible results from the first session.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid sun exposure for 8 days.
  • Do not use depigmenting products, only moisturizer and sunscreen.
  • Avoid saunas, steam rooms, and humid areas.

Contraindications

  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding.
  • Allergies to the components.
  • Recent use of isotretinoin or retinoids.
