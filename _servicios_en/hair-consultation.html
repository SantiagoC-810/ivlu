Our complete hair diagnostic process

The purpose of the consultation is to recommend the most appropriate treatment based on the diagnosis, which may include topical treatments, oral medications, hair therapies, or changes in habits and diet.

If significant hair loss is detected and other treatments have not been effective, hair transplantation is an option. This surgical procedure transplants healthy follicles from a donor area to regions with hair loss, offering a lasting and natural solution.
