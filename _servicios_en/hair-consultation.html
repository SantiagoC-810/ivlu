Our complete hair diagnosis process

The goal of the consultation is to recommend the most appropriate treatment according to the diagnosis, which may include topical treatments, oral medications, hair therapies, or changes in habits and diet.

If significant hair loss is detected and other treatments have not been effective, hair implantation is an option. This surgical procedure transplants healthy follicles from a donor area to regions with hair loss, offering a lasting and natural solution.
