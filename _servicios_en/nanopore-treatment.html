How Does It Work?

The Nanopore treatment creates microchannels in the skin using microneedles, facilitating the absorption of nutrients. This process stimulates cell regeneration and collagen production, improving the overall appearance of the skin and promoting rejuvenation.

Treatment Protocol

  • Initial Consultation: A dermatological evaluation is conducted to ensure the treatment is suitable for you.
  • Number of Sessions: A cycle of 3 sessions is recommended, with one-month intervals for optimal results.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid Sun Exposure: It is crucial to protect your skin from the sun for a week after the treatment.
  • Use Sunscreen: Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen every 3 hours.
  • Hydration: Keep your skin hydrated with recommended products.
  • Recovery: For 2-5 days, your skin may show redness and flaking, which is part of the regeneration process.
