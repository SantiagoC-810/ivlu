Hair transplant: the definitive solution for hair loss

Hair transplantation is a surgical procedure that involves transferring healthy hair follicles from a donor area to regions with hair loss. It's an effective option for restoring density in specific areas, offering natural and long-lasting results.

Benefits of Hair Transplantation

01Restore volume in thinning areas: Ideal for areas with advanced hair loss, improving hair density.

Phases of Hair Transplantation

01Assessment and Design: The scalp is analyzed and a personalized plan is designed.

02Extraction and Implantation: Follicles are extracted one by one and implanted in the recipient areas.

03Results and Follow-up: At our IVLU Medical Center, follow-up is carried out to ensure the success and healthy growth of the grafts.
