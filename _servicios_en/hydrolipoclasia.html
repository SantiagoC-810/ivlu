Everything you need to know about advanced hydrolipoclasia

Advanced hydrolipoclasia combines the best of non-invasive aesthetic medicine to effectively target localized fat resistant to diets and exercise. At IVLU, we incorporate the most advanced protocols to maximize results and minimize discomfort.

01The procedure begins with a complete medical evaluation and body mapping to precisely identify the areas to be treated. We use diagnostic ultrasound to measure the exact thickness of the fat layer, allowing us to personalize the treatment according to your specific characteristics.

02During the session, a personalized hypoosmotic solution is infiltrated through virtually painless microinjections in the area to be treated. Subsequently, we apply high-intensity focused ultrasound that causes lysis (rupture) of fat cells, allowing their content to be naturally metabolized and eliminated by the body.

03The procedure lasts approximately 45-60 minutes and is performed on an outpatient basis with topical anesthesia. You can return to your normal activities immediately, although we recommend avoiding intense exercise for 48 hours and maintaining adequate hydration to optimize the elimination process.

04To enhance results, we include a post-treatment lymphatic drainage and the application of shock waves that promote the elimination of released fatty acids and prevent the appearance of fibrosis. Additionally, we use specific compression garments that favor skin retraction.

05Results are progressive: you will notice a visible reduction from the first session, but the maximum effect is observed between 15-30 days afterward. The complete protocol consists of 4-6 sessions spaced every 15 days. Once completed, the results are long-lasting as long as a healthy lifestyle is maintained.
