Advanced Radiesse® Technology

Radiesse® combines calcium hydroxylapatite (CaHA) microspheres suspended in a biocompatible gel for deep bio-stimulation.

01Exclusive Composition CaHA microspheres (30%) and carrier gel (70%) provide ideal viscosity for maximum tissue projection.

02Dual Mechanism The gel offers immediate volume while microspheres stimulate type I collagen production for long-lasting dermal remodeling.

033D Vectorization We apply Radiesse following specific vectors for natural lifting without compromising facial expressiveness.

04Body Hyperdilution We use dilutions (1:2 or 1:4) to treat laxity in large areas like arms, abdomen and thighs.

05Biodegradability Radiesse naturally breaks down in 12-15 months without residues, ensuring complete biocompatibility.
