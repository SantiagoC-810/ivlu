Profhilo®: Advanced Bioremodeling for Radiant Skin

Profhilo® is an innovative treatment that uses ultra-pure, highly concentrated hyaluronic acid (64 mg) in a combination of high and low molecular weight, created through an exclusive thermal production process. It's designed for skin bioremodeling on the face, neck, and décolletage.

What does the treatment involve?

Profhilo® is administered using the Bio Aesthetic Points technique. This injectable procedure improves skin laxity, smooths fine lines, and provides luminosity, deep hydration, and rejuvenation. Its unique formula corrects natural or induced skin depressions, providing a tightening and revitalizing effect.
