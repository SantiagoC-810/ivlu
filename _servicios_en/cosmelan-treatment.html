Cosmelan®: Effective Treatment for Facial Spots

Facial spots are common pigmentation disorders caused by excessive melanin production, the skin's main pigment. At IVLU, we use advanced technology and effective methods to safely treat facial pigmentation.

How does the Cosmelan® treatment work?

Cosmelan® is a depigmenting treatment that works quickly and effectively, removing or reducing facial spots while evening out skin tone. Suitable for all skin types.

Mechanism of action

Cosmelan® inhibits the tyrosinase enzyme responsible for melanin production, gradually lightening skin and unifying tone.

Procedure

The treatment involves professional applications using facial masks and massage techniques that enhance absorption of depigmenting actives. A prior evaluation is necessary to determine eligibility and design a personalized plan.
