In this section, you’ll find detailed guidance on what to expect before, during, and after your treatment at IVLU. We’re committed to your well-being and ensure you have all the tools for an optimal experience.

Facial Treatment Success Stories

Hair Restoration Success Stories

Hair Transplant

A surgical aesthetic procedure that transplants healthy hair follicles from donor areas (typically the back of the head) to thinning or balding areas for permanent, natural-looking results.

Hair Regrowth Therapy

Designed to slow hair loss, increase density, and improve hair quality using non-surgical methods like PRP and laser therapy. Ideal for early-stage hair thinning or as maintenance after transplants. Results vary and require periodic sessions.
