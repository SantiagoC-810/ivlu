Our Story and Philosophy

IVLU was founded by Dr. Ivonne Camacho and Dr. Luisa Antia, who after years of experience in prestigious clinics, created in 2023 a space that combines the most advanced technology with a deeply human and personalized approach to aesthetic medicine and hair restoration.

Our philosophy is based on three pillars: medical excellence, natural results, and exceptional experience. We believe in enhancing each person's genuine beauty, not transforming it. Every treatment we perform aims to enhance the natural harmony of face and body, respecting the unique characteristics that define each patient.

What Sets Us Apart

Exclusively Medical Approach

Unlike many aesthetic centers, at IVLU all treatments are performed by expert physicians. This guarantees not only superior results but also the highest safety standards.

Cutting-Edge Technology

We work exclusively with internationally certified equipment and products, carefully selecting each technology after evaluating its effectiveness and safety profile. We stay constantly updated with the latest medical-aesthetic innovations.

Premium Personalized Care

Our care model prioritizes quality time with each patient. Consultations are extensive and detailed, allowing us to fully understand your needs and design truly personalized protocols.

Proven Natural Results

Our specialty is achieving results that enhance your natural beauty without showing intervention. We want you to look like your best self, not like someone else, respecting the natural harmony and proportions of your face and body.

Dr. Luisa Antia

Dr. Luisa Antia is a surgeon graduated from El Bosque University and aesthetic physician trained at the University of Tolima. Dr. Antia has completed multiple specialized courses and updates in injectables and facial harmonization, allowing her to offer patients state-of-the-art treatments with natural and personalized results.

Her comprehensive and personalized approach has allowed her to perfect advanced techniques that combine the best of aesthetic medicine, always with a commitment to patient health and wellbeing.

Dr. Antia is a member of the Non-Surgical Facial Rejuvenation Society (SIRF), which keeps her at the forefront of the latest innovations in non-invasive aesthetic procedures, ensuring long-lasting results.
