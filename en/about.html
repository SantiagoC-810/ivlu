Get to Know Us

IVLU: Premium Aesthetic Medicine and Hair Restoration Clinic in Bogotá

Our commitment: natural results with medical excellence

Our Story and Philosophy

IVLU was born from the vision of Dr. Ivon and Dr. Luisa, who after years of experience in prestigious international clinics, decided to create a space in 2018 that combines the most advanced technology with a deeply human and personalized approach to aesthetic medicine and hair restoration.

Our philosophy is built on three pillars: medical excellence, natural results, and exceptional experience. We believe in enhancing each person's genuine beauty, not transforming it. Every treatment we perform aims to enhance the natural harmony of the face and body, respecting the unique characteristics that define each patient.

What Sets Us Apart

Exclusively Medical Approach

Unlike many aesthetic centers, at IVLU all treatments are performed by expert physicians with international training. This ensures not only superior results but also the highest safety standards.

Cutting-Edge Technology

We work exclusively with internationally certified equipment and products, carefully selecting each technology after evaluating its effectiveness and safety profile. We constantly stay updated with the latest medical-aesthetic innovations.

Premium Personalized Care

Our care model prioritizes quality time with each patient. Consultations are extensive and detailed, allowing us to fully understand your needs and design truly personalized protocols.

Proven Natural Results

Our specialty is achieving results that enhance your natural beauty without showing evidence of intervention. We want you to look like your best self, not like someone else, respecting the harmony and natural proportions of your face and body.

Our Team of Medical Experts

Dr. Ivon Valencia

Medical expert in Aesthetic Medicine and Hair Restoration

Dr. Ivon graduated with honors from the National University of Colombia and completed her specialization in Aesthetic Medicine and Anti-aging at the University of Barcelona. She has more than 12 years of experience and multiple international certifications in advanced facial and body rejuvenation techniques.

She has trained with the world's most recognized experts in techniques such as MD Codes, HiFu, and advanced biostimulation. Her approach is characterized by achieving natural and harmonious results through detailed and personalized facial analysis.

  • Member of the Colombian Society of Aesthetic Medicine
  • Certified in advanced injectable techniques by Allergan Medical Institute
  • Master in Tissue Regeneration Techniques, Complutense University of Madrid
  • International instructor in biostimulation and botulinum toxin techniques

Dr. Luisa Antia

Medical expert in Trichology and Hair Restoration

Dr. Luisa graduated from the University of the Andes and is a expert in Trichology and Hair Restoration from the Autonomous University of Barcelona. She has more than 10 years of experience dedicated exclusively to the diagnosis and treatment of hair disorders.

She has specialized in the most advanced techniques of non-surgical and surgical hair restoration, including FUE HD micrografting. Her passion for research keeps her at the forefront of innovative treatments for alopecia.

  • Member of the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (ISHRS)
  • Certified in FUE HD hair micrografting
  • Diploma in Clinical and Surgical Trichology, University of Alcalá
  • Researcher in regenerative hair therapies with stem cells
