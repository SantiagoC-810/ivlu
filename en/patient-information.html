Patient Information

This comprehensive guide details what to expect before, during, and after your treatment at IVLU. We're committed to your safety and satisfaction, providing all necessary information for an optimal experience.

Pre-Treatment Preparation

First Consultation Guidelines

Maximize your initial consultation by following these recommendations:

  • Clearly define your aesthetic goals and expectations
  • Prepare a list of questions or concerns
  • Document medical history, allergies, and current medications
  • Avoid alcohol consumption 24 hours prior
  • Discontinue anti-inflammatory medications and blood thinners 7 days prior (with medical approval)
  • Bring relevant previous medical records
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment

Initial Evaluation Process

Your 45-60 minute consultation includes:

  • Detailed discussion of goals, medical history, and lifestyle
  • Physical examination of treatment areas
  • Digital facial/hair analysis
  • Photographic documentation for progress tracking
  • Personalized treatment plan presentation
  • Comprehensive Q&A session
  • Customized pricing proposal

Treatment-Specific Preparation

Each procedure requires specific preparations for optimal results and safety:

Neuromodulators (Dysport/Xeomin)

Preparation for wrinkle relaxers:

  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours prior
  • Discontinue blood-thinning supplements (garlic, ginkgo, vitamin E) 7 days prior
  • No strenuous exercise on treatment day
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin
  • Disclose previous neuromodulator treatments

Dermal Fillers & Biostimulators

For hyaluronic acid, Radiesse, Sculptra, or Profhilo:

  • Suspend blood thinners 7 days prior (with medical approval)
  • Avoid alcohol 48 hours before treatment
  • Discontinue chemical peels 7-10 days prior
  • Report history of cold sores for lip treatments
  • Arrive without makeup

Hair Restoration

For FUE transplants and scalp therapies:

  • Complete pre-operative testing if required
  • Avoid chemical treatments 2 weeks prior
  • Discontinue Minoxidil 7 days pre-transplant
  • Wash hair with neutral shampoo day of procedure
  • Avoid nicotine and energy drinks 48 hours prior
  • Wear front-opening clothing
  • Arrange transportation home

Your safety is our priority. Full disclosure of medical conditions, allergies, medications, and previous treatments is crucial for personalized care and optimal results.

During Your Treatment

Treatment Experience

Comfort levels vary by procedure type:

  • Neuromodulators: Brief pinching sensation, typically requiring no anesthesia
  • Dermal Fillers: Mild discomfort managed with topical anesthetics
  • Collagen Stimulators: Pressure sensation with advanced application techniques
  • Hair Transplantation: Comfort maintained through tumescent anesthesia
  • Regenerative Therapies: Minimal discomfort with brief pressure sensations

Our medical experts continuously monitor comfort levels, adjusting techniques as needed. Many patients relax completely during longer procedures.

Treatment Durations

Plan accordingly for your appointment:

  • Neuromodulators: 15-30 minutes
  • Dermal Fillers: 30-60 minutes
  • Collagen Stimulators: 45-90 minutes
  • Hair Transplantation: 4-8 hours
  • Non-Surgical Hair Therapies: 60-90 minutes
  • Advanced Skin Tightening: 60-120 minutes

Times include preparation, procedure, and post-care instructions.

Post-Treatment Care

General Aftercare

Essential guidelines for most procedures:

  • Avoid sun exposure for 48-72 hours
  • No strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours
  • Avoid extreme temperatures (saunas/ice packs)
  • Refrain from alcohol for 24-48 hours
  • Don't manipulate treated areas
  • Use SPF 50+ daily
  • Follow customized skincare regimen
  • Maintain proper hydration

Common Side Effects

Temporary reactions typically resolve within days:

  • Redness: 24-48 hour duration
  • Mild Swelling: 2-5 days (common with fillers)
  • Bruising: 3-10 days in vascular areas
  • Tenderness: Gradually diminishes

Unusual or persistent reactions require immediate notification. Direct expert access provided for recovery support.

Emergency Protocol

Contact us immediately if experiencing:

  • Asymmetric or excessive swelling
  • Throbbing pain
  • Skin discoloration
  • Fever or malaise
  • Any concerning symptoms

24/7 emergency support available for all patients.

Treatment-Specific Aftercare

Neuromodulators

Optimize results with:

  • Maintain upright position 4 hours post-treatment
  • Exercise treated muscles first day
  • Avoid facial pressure/massage 48 hours
  • No tight eyewear if treating glabellar area

Dermal Fillers

For optimal outcomes:

  • Apply cool compresses first 24-48 hours
  • Elevate head while sleeping
  • Avoid makeup 12-24 hours
  • Delay dental work 2 weeks for lip treatments

Hair Restoration

Post-transplant care:

  • Follow specialized washing protocol
  • Sleep elevated first 3-5 nights
  • Avoid hats/helmets until cleared
  • No sweating-inducing activities 7-10 days

Your commitment to aftercare directly impacts treatment success. Our team provides continuous support throughout recovery – your satisfaction remains our ultimate priority.

Follow-Up & Results Timeline

Clinical Follow-Up Protocol

Comprehensive monitoring ensures optimal outcomes:

  • 24-48 hour wellness check
  • 7-14 day follow-up appointment
  • Comparative photographic analysis
  • Results evaluation and adjustments
  • Maintenance scheduling

International patients receive virtual follow-ups via secure video consultations.

Expected Results Timeline

Treatment-specific outcome development:

  • Neuromodulators: Initial effect 24-72hrs, peak at 7-10 days
  • Hyaluronic Fillers: Immediate results, final look at 2 weeks
  • Radiesse: Progressive improvement over 2-3 months
  • Sculptra: Visible results at 6-8 weeks, optimal after 2-3 sessions
  • Hair Transplant: Transient shedding at 3 weeks, growth from 3-4 months, final results at 12 months

Individual results vary based on age, skin quality, metabolism, and aftercare compliance.
