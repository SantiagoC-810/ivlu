About IVLU Clinic

What makes IVLU different from other aesthetic clinics?

IVLU stands apart through three core principles: 1) Physician-led care: All treatments are administered by board-certified medical doctors, not aestheticians. 2) Premium technology: We use only FDA/CE-certified devices and premium-grade products. 3) Personalized approach: Our extended consultations allow customized treatment plans that enhance your natural beauty with subtle, transformative results. We specialize in achieving "touched-by-expertise" outcomes that never look overdone.

Are your physicians properly certified?

Yes. Our medical team holds specialized certifications in aesthetic medicine and hair restoration. Founders Dr. Ivon and Dr. Luisa maintain active memberships in international professional associations including the International Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ISAPS) and American Academy of Dermatology (AAD). Review their credentials in our About Us section.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

We provide initial virtual consultations for international patients. These 30-minute video sessions allow our specialists to review your concerns and suggest treatment options. However, most procedures require an in-person evaluation for precise diagnosis and planning. We recommend scheduling your virtual consultation 2-3 weeks before your Bogotá visit.

Treatment Information

What treatments do you offer?

Our premium services include:

  • Facial Rejuvenation: Neurotoxins (Dysport/Xeomin), HA fillers, collagen stimulators (Radiesse®, Sculptra®, Profhilo®), thread lifts, and regenerative therapies
  • Hair Restoration: FUE HD Hair Transplantation, PRP therapy, stem cell treatments, medical hair loss programs
  • Body Contouring: Fat reduction, skin tightening, hyperhidrosis treatment

During your consultation, we'll design a personalized treatment plan addressing your specific concerns and goals.

Are treatments painful?

We prioritize comfort using advanced techniques:

  • Topical anesthetics for injectables
  • Ultra-fine needles (33G-36G)
  • Tumescent anesthesia for hair transplants
  • Vibration distraction devices

Most patients report minimal discomfort - typically described as brief pressure or mild pinching. Our medical team continuously monitors comfort levels throughout procedures.

How safe are your treatments?

Safety is paramount. Our protocols include:

  • Full medical history review
  • FDA/CE-approved products only
  • Sterile technique compliance
  • Emergency preparedness training

We maintain a perfect safety record with zero major complications since our founding. All potential risks are thoroughly discussed during consultations.

How long do results last?

Duration varies by treatment:

  • Neurotoxins: 3-4 months
  • HA Fillers: 9-18 months
  • Sculptra®: 2+ years
  • Hair Transplantation: Permanent
  • Ultrasound Skin Tightening: 12-18 months

We provide personalized maintenance plans to optimize longevity. Lifestyle factors and individual metabolism affect duration.
