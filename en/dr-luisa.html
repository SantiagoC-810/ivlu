Dr. Luisa Antia

Dr. Luisa is a medical surgeon who graduated from El Bosque University and an aesthetic physician trained at the University of Tolima. With over 5 years of experience in injectable treatments, facial harmonization, and cutting-edge technologies, she has distinguished herself as one of the most committed experts in non-surgical facial rejuvenation.

Dr. Luisa Antia has completed multiple advanced training courses specialized in injectables and facial harmonization, allowing her to offer her patients state-of-the-art treatments with natural and personalized results.

Biography

Her comprehensive and personalized approach has allowed her to perfect advanced techniques that combine the best of aesthetic medicine, always with a commitment to the health and well-being of her patients.

Dr. Luisa Antia is a member of the Society for Non-Surgical Facial Rejuvenation (SIRF), which allows her to stay at the forefront of the latest innovations in non-invasive aesthetic procedures, guaranteeing long-lasting results.
