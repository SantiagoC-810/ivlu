Lumecca: Intense pulsed light technology

Intense pulsed light (IPL) is a phototherapy technology that uses a wide range of light wavelengths to treat various skin conditions.

Unlike laser, which emits a single wavelength, pulsed light emits pulses of light with multiple wavelengths, making it more versatile for treating different skin problems.

How does it work?

Intense pulsed light is applied to the skin using a device that emits pulses of light. This light is absorbed by pigmented cells (such as those that cause spots and wrinkles) or by dilated blood vessels. When absorbed, the light is transformed into heat, which destroys unwanted cells or stimulates collagen production, improving the appearance of the skin.
